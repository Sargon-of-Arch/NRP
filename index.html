Welcome

We are the National Reform Organization. We advocate for radical political and social reform in Iraq. Our mission is to revive heritage, protect the abused, and challenge corruption — from the grassroots to the highest institutions.

Language Revival Project

Our initiative aims to revive the ancient Akkadian language using modern tools and scripts like Syriac. It's not just culture — it's resistance through identity, memory, and permanence.

Our Goals

  • Expose and resist political corruption.
  • Protect women, children, and minorities from abuse and silencing.
  • Revive Mesopotamian heritage and dignity through language and culture.
  • Build a new national conscience — one rooted in truth, accountability, and strength.

Whistleblow / Report Abuse

If you've witnessed or experienced abuse, especially involving government agents or militias, contact us securely. Your voice matters.
