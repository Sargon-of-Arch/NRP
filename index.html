Our Mission

To unite and awaken Mesopotamia’s remnants, push back against tyranny, and demand dignity for the abused and voiceless. Through activism, media, and culture, we shape a new force that speaks Akkadian in code and truth in rage.

Language Revival

The Akkadian language, reborn through the Syriac script — a poetic resurrection of our roots. Join us in restoring its vocabulary, syntax, and soul.
